### What is Innovation Enablement?

Innovation Enablement is the function that makes it easier for others in the organization to innovate. Instead of doing all innovation centrally, enablement teams:
- Provide tools, platforms, and frameworks
- Reduce barriers to experimentation
- Democratize access to emerging technologies
- Create reusable components and patterns
- Train and coach others on innovation methods

### "How do you think about AI governance?"

> "AI governance is about enabling, not blocking. The best governance frameworks I've built include:
>
> - **Explainability requirements** calibrated to the decision impact - higher stakes means more transparency
> - **Fairness testing** before deployment and ongoing monitoring after
> - **Model documentation** that serves auditors, operators, and future developers
> - **Clear escalation paths** for edge cases and failures
>
> In regulated industries like insurance, this isn't optional. But I've found that good governance actually accelerates deployment by building stakeholder trust and reducing late-stage surprises."

### "How would you work with our AI and Automation teams?"

> "I see R&D's role as complementary to these teams. R&D explores the frontier - testing emerging technologies, running experiments, de-risking new approaches. AI and Automation teams operationalize proven approaches at scale.
>
> The collaboration model I'd propose:
> - **Early alignment** on strategic priorities so R&D explores what matters
> - **Regular sharing** of R&D learnings, even from failed experiments
> - **Clear handoff criteria** for when something moves from R&D to production teams
> - **Feedback loops** so we learn from what works and doesn't work at scale
>
> I've both led these functions and partnered with them as a stakeholder. I speak the language and understand the constraints they operate under."

### "What's your experience with intelligent automation?"

> "Intelligent automation - combining AI with process automation - is where I see the biggest near-term ROI for most organizations.
